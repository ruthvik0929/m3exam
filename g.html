List objects have a sort() method that will sort the list
alphanumerically, ascending, by default:

num=[5,7,2,1,8,3]
num.sort()
print(num)

List objects have a sort() method that will sort the list
alphanumerically, descending, by default:

num=[5,7,2,1,8,3]
num.sort(reverse=True)
print(num)

List Slicing and Indexing

students=["vijay", "aarya", "akhil", "manoj", "sanjana"]
print("First three students:", students[:3])
print("Last two students:", students[3:])
